Star Wars

Star Wars did not play an important role in my life until my teenage years. After I finished middle school my parents decided to make me go to Design Tech. Design Tech is far from my house and I only knew three kids. I was still able to make friends though. Near the end of the year my friends changed so I started drifting away. Over the 2019 summer I was lonely so I started re watching the Star Wars movies. Then the tv shows. Then I started reading comics and other materials. And then I started watching youtube videos. I got really into the lore so I started building mocs. I built a couple but ended up throwing them out because I got bored of Star Wars. What I took away was that I liked to build and engineer things and now I have an idea what I want to do with my life.
